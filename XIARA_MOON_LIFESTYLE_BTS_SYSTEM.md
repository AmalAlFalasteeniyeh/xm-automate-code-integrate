## THE LIFESTYLE FORMULA

```
[XIARA_CORE] + [EVERYDAY_ACTIVITY] + [CASUAL_SETTING] +
[NATURAL_POSE] + [AUTHENTIC_EXPRESSION] + [RELATABLE_DETAIL] +
[ANTI-MODEL_GUARDS]
```

### The Core Components:

1. **XIARA_CORE** - Always include physical characteristics
2. **EVERYDAY_ACTIVITY** - Real things people do (coffee, Netflix, getting ready)
3. **CASUAL_SETTING** - Real spaces with lived-in details
4. **NATURAL_POSE** - Not posed, caught in action
5. **AUTHENTIC_EXPRESSION** - Real emotions not performed
6. **RELATABLE_DETAIL** - Messy hair, unmade bed, coffee cups
7. **ANTI-MODEL_GUARDS** - Explicit instructions to keep it real

## üé® LIFESTYLE COMPONENT LIBRARY

### Casual Settings (Always Include Lived-In Details):
- unmade bed with sheets tangled and pillows askew
- kitchen counter with breakfast mess and coffee maker
- bathroom with products scattered across counter
- couch with throw pillows displaced and blankets bunched
- bedroom floor with clothes pile in corner
- coffee shop corner with laptop and empty cups
- car interior dashboard visible (parked selfie)
- hotel room post-check-in with suitcase open
- desk with papers and sticky notes everywhere
- closet door open with clothes visible

### Natural Poses (Not Performed):
- mid-movement caught in action
- looking at phone screen not camera
- legs tucked up on couch casually
- sitting cross-legged on floor
- leaning against counter supporting self
- sprawled on bed in natural position
- standing casually with hip popped
- walking toward camera naturally
- bent over task fully focused
- stretching with eyes closed

### Authentic Expressions (Real Emotions):
- genuine laugh with eyes closed
- focused concentration on task at hand
- sleepy tired smile, barely awake
- goofy playful face, personality showing
- mid-yawn caught naturally
- confused/thinking expression, problem solving
- relieved/content sigh after accomplishment
- excited surprise, genuine reaction
- frustrated but laughing at mistake
- peaceful serene meditation face

### Relatable Details (Connection Builders):
- messy hair in bun with flyaways
- minimal or no makeup showing real skin
- glasses instead of contacts for comfort
- coffee cup or water bottle in hand
- phone charging cable visible plugged in
- laundry basket visible in background
- takeout containers and utensils
- nail polish remover and cotton balls scattered
- hair ties on wrist
- unmade bed with pillows everywhere
- shopping bags still unpacked

### Clothing Reality (What She Actually Wears):
- oversized hoodies and sweatshirts
- boyfriend jeans or mom jeans
- leggings and sports bra post-workout
- sleep shorts and tank top
- robe or towel after shower
- loungewear sets for comfort
- actual pajamas not lingerie
- worn-in t-shirts with logos
- comfortable bralettes not push-up
- flip flops or barefoot at home
- messy hair tie ponytail
- no bra lazy day vibes

## üö´ ANTI-MODEL GUARDS

**ADD THESE TO EVERY LIFESTYLE PROMPT:**

### Mandatory Phrases:
- "not posed for camera, candid moment"
- "natural expression not seductive"
- "real apartment/home setting with lived-in details"
- "iPhone selfie quality not professional camera"
- "messy background showing real life"
- "caught in actual activity not staged"
- "genuine emotion not performed"
- "lifestyle photography not fashion editorial"
- "not looking at camera, focused on task"
- "mid-process not finished/polished"

### What to Avoid:
- Perfect lighting setups
- Clean, styled backgrounds
- Professional camera quality
- Posed body positioning
- Performed seductive expressions
- Fashion editorial aesthetic
- Studio backdrop feeling
- "Instagram perfect" vibe
- Everything matching and coordinated
- No signs of real life visible

## üé¨ EXECUTION NOTES

### When Generating Lifestyle Content:

1. **Think Documentary Not Photoshoot**
   - What would phone camera catch?
   - What does real moment look like?
   - How does genuine expression appear?

2. **Embrace the Mess**
   - Perfection = distance
   - Mess = relatability
   - Real life isn't Instagram perfect

3. **Capture Process Not Result**
   - Mid-makeup application
   - Half-styled hair
   - Cooking in progress
   - Getting ready journey

4. **Show Vulnerability**
   - Tired moments
   - Frustrated expressions
   - Silly mistakes
   - Genuine struggles

5. **Let Personality Shine**
   - Goofy faces okay
   - Weird habits visible
   - Real interests shown
   - Authentic reactions
